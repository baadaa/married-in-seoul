She Ends Up in New York

A handful years into her career, the twenty-something-year-old nurse began to feel her life wasn’t entirely satisfying. To reach a next level in her career and—more importantly—to lead a more fulfilling life, Young-Mi decided to study and work in America. Through a few years of hard work, she received her degrees and got a job in New York.
