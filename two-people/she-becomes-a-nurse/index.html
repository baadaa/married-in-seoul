She Becomes a Nurse

Growing up big-hearted through the years, Young-Mi entered a nursing college right after high school. While she didn’t originally aspired to be a nurse, she had always loved to be in service of others, and becoming a nurse gave her an opportunity to do just that and feel fulfilled.
